About Me

Sarah Jones is an American art student. Her work includes a variety of different mediums, but centers on functional 3-D design (such as metalwork, woodwork & textile) and some 2-D design (drawing & painting).

Her inspirations come from nature, fairytales, folklore, and her New York surroundings. Traveling is another source of inspiration. She has currently had the opportunity to travel to visit five countries, and most notably spent a semester studying abroad in Lithuania

She opened her etsy shop, "Broken Fairytales", in 2012. It sells mostly fantasy and literature themed items from mugs to clothes to jewelry.

Her hobbies include an obsessive amount of reading, writing, tea-drinking, and nerd culture.

She lives vicariously through her three cats, dog, and parrot.
